# Sumary: 

The Peer Tutoring Platform was developed to help students easily find academic support from peer tutors within the university. The system allows users to register and log in to access the platform.  

Students can search for tutors based on subjects and view tutor profiles to understand their expertise, hourly rate, and availability. The platform also allows students to book tutoring sessions by selecting a suitable date and time slot.  

Tutors can manage their profiles by adding subjects, setting their hourly rate, updating their biography, and managing their availability. They can also accept or reject booking requests from students.  

In addition, the platform includes a rating system where students can rate tutors after completing a session. This helps maintain the quality of tutoring services and allows other students to choose tutors based on feedback.  

Overall, the system provides a simple and organized way for students to connect with peer tutors and receive academic support.
